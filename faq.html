Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

A list of commonly asked questions and responses. If you have a question that is not addressed below, Contact Us.

  1. How can I join the group?
    • Navigate to the Louisiana Cosplayers Facebook Group and press Join. An Admin will process your request.
  2. Why has my request not been approved yet?
    • We receive requests on a daily basis and processing them takes time. Please be patient.
  3. How can I become more involved in the group or group events?
    • Come to our Events, ask and respond to questions in the Facebook group. Or Fill out your information on our LAC Moderator/Volunteer Application Form.
  4. Why was my post/comment/picture removed from the page?
    • Post/comment/picture removal is decided by the team if it breaks the Rules.
  5. Who do I contact about being harassed by another member during a Louisiana Cosplayers event?
    • Fill out as much detail as possible on our LAC Incident Report Form and we will handle the situation to the best of our abilities.
