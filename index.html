About Us

Louisiana Cosplayers is an organization focused on discussing cosplay, helping each other, and hosting meetups. Our members actively post in our Facebook Group about a large range of cosplay topics including: progress, help with design concepts, help with construction, convention meetups, locations to buy cosplay and cosplay accessories.

All cosplayers are welcome here: Anime, Novels, Manga, Video Games, Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Steampunk, etc!

Join us at any of our upcoming Events or Conventions!
